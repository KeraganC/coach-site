LIFE IS BEST LIVED IN COMMUNITY

Helping you cultivate connection that feels peaceful, not performative.

Hi, I'm Keragan.

I’m a social health and connection coach whose mission it is to help others connect better. With themselves, with their friends, with their community.

Not to be dramatic (okay, maybe just a touch), but connection is actually a matter of life and death.* It’s something we all crave. But it’s much like that one flakey friend — coming and going as they please, reliable one second, nowhere to be seen the next. Our social connections are all at once the source of our most joyful moments, and our most despondent.

The quality of your relationships determines the quality of your life. What we believe is possible for ourselves is directly related to the people and places we spend time with and in. No pressure.

I’m here to help you take ownership over your connection. Imagine a world where you leave social settings feeling more aligned, energized, and expressed.

If this resonates with you, let's connect!

Keragan

What we'll explore together

Connection to self

What brings you to life? When do you feel most yourself? What parts of you are waiting to be expressed? We start by getting honest about who you are beneath the roles, expectations, and self-protection.

Connection with others

Friendship, intimacy, repair, conflict, vulnerability, and the everyday courage of letting yourself be seen in your many forms. Navigating the messiness that comes with being in relationship.

Belonging & community

Finding, creating, and tending the spaces where you feel seen, energized, useful, and rooted. Diversifying your opportunities for connection.

Relational narratives

The beliefs and habits that shape how you show up in relationships. Do you have a growth or fixed mindset? Do you keep the peace at the expense of your needs and wants?

Social courage

Initiating plans, asking for what you want, taking up space, facing discomfort, and leading with action.

Place, ritual & rhythm

Leveraging neighborhood clubs, third spaces, and routines that make connection more accessible, reliable, and sustainable.

Peek into my mind (if you dare)

Personal essays on navigating our inner and outer worlds with courage, honesty, and an open heart.
